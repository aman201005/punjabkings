Our Story

Welcome to the official "About Us" page of the Punjab Kings! Formerly known as Kings XI Punjab, we are a passionate and determined franchise cricket team competing in the prestigious Indian Premier League (IPL). Based in Mullanpur, Mohali, Punjab, our journey in the IPL has been filled with exciting moments, dedicated fans, and a constant pursuit of excellence.

The Punjab Kings franchise was established in [Year of Establishment] and has since become an integral part of the IPL landscape. We are owned by a consortium of passionate individuals and strive to bring thrilling cricket action to our supporters across the globe.

Our Journey in the IPL

Over the years, the Punjab Kings have showcased a blend of talented players, strategic gameplay, and unwavering team spirit. We reached the IPL final in 2014 and have made other playoff appearances, consistently aiming for the coveted IPL trophy. Our rebranding to Punjab Kings in 2021 marked a new chapter, reinforcing our connection with the vibrant state of Punjab and our ambition for future success.

We are committed to building a strong and competitive team that resonates with our fans. Our focus extends beyond the field, as we actively engage with our community and strive to promote the spirit of cricket.

Our Home Ground

Our primary home ground for the IPL is the state-of-the-art Maharaja Yadavindra Singh International Cricket Stadium in Mullanpur, Chandigarh. This modern facility provides a fantastic atmosphere for our home matches and allows us to connect with our passionate fanbase in the region. We have also historically played matches at the scenic Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association Stadium in Dharamsala.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading franchise in the IPL, known for our competitive spirit, strong team culture, and deep connection with our fans. We are dedicated to playing an exciting brand of cricket and achieving sustained success in the league.
